Personalized Content Recommendations – UX AI

Delivering tailored user experiences using AI-driven content strategies.

Project Overview

This project focuses on designing AI-powered recommendation systems that personalize content based on user behavior, preferences, and engagement history.

Objectives

  • Enhance user engagement through personalized content delivery
  • Increase retention rates with adaptive content suggestions
  • Design UX that makes recommendations intuitive and non-intrusive
  • Incorporate AI insights without compromising transparency

Methodology

Data analysis of user interactions, prototyping personalized feeds, iterative usability testing, and implementing AI-based content ranking strategies for improved UX.

Deliverables

  • Personalized content feed mockups
  • Interactive prototype demonstrating AI recommendations
  • Usability testing reports with actionable insights
  • AI UX guidelines for future product scalability

Outcome

Users experienced higher engagement and satisfaction due to relevant content recommendations, showing the impact of thoughtful AI integration in UX design.
