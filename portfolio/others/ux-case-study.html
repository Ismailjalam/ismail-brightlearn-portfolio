UX Case Study Presentation

Transforming research insights into meaningful design decisions

1️⃣ Problem Definition

Users of the mobile banking app were abandoning transactions midway due to unclear feedback and complex flow. The business needed a simpler, trust-building experience that increases completion rates.

2️⃣ Research Process

  • User interviews with 12 frequent mobile banking users
  • Usability testing with 8 participants on prototype flows
  • Heuristic evaluation and competitor benchmarking

Key findings revealed that users wanted instant confirmations, clearer progress indicators, and accessible button placements.

3️⃣ Design Solution

The redesign focused on creating trust through simplicity and predictability. New elements included:

  • Progress bar for transaction tracking
  • Clear confirmation modals and success animations
  • Streamlined button hierarchy with consistent icons

4️⃣ Prototype & Testing

Interactive prototype built in Figma and tested using Maze analytics. Average completion rate increased from 62% to 91%.

View Interactive Prototype

5️⃣ Final Outcome

The redesigned interface improved clarity, reduced cognitive load, and built user confidence. The final case study presentation included insights visualization, storytelling, and a high-fidelity clickable prototype.
